Sailor Piece Progress Trackers FAQ
Do these trackers save between visits?
Yes. The progress trackers save in your browser local storage, so they stay on your device and sync between the related wiki pages.
Which trackers are shared across pages?
Boss Rush, Infinite Tower, Skill Tree, and the endgame account checklist use shared saved values, so updating one of those trackers also updates the same tracker wherever else it appears on the site.
