Overview

Cosmic Being

  • Cosmic Being is one of the newly tracked Sea 2 fighting styles.
  • Cosmic Being can be obtained either by trading materials to the Cosmic Being NPC on Punch Island or by defeating the Garou world boss tied to the route.
  • The NPC route requires the Cosmic Being title, 12 Cosmic Essence, 8 Star Mark, 5 Galaxy Shard, and 2 Monster Pulse.
  • Garou has 499,999,997,952 HP and drops Cosmic Essence, Star Mark, Galaxy Shard, Cosmic Body, Monster Pulse, Cosmic Being, Guild Key, Aura Crate, and Cosmetic Crate.
  • The tracked Garou row lists Cosmic Being at 0.525%, which this page treats as the direct style unlock chance.
  • Garou appears 30 minutes after server start and then 30 minutes after the previous kill, while also using an adaptation mechanic at each 25% HP lost.
  • Cosmic Being also has a separate Cosmic Master questline for the F move: defeat the world boss 15 times, reach Infinite Tower floor 90+ five times, and obtain Astral Bloodline.
Section

How to get Cosmic Being

  • Cosmic Being can be obtained either by talking to the Cosmic Being NPC on top of a building in Punch Island and trading the required materials listed below, or by defeating the Garou world boss in the middle of Punch Island.
  • The NPC trade route uses the confirmed requirement bundle: Cosmic Being title, 12 Cosmic Essence, 8 Star Mark, 5 Galaxy Shard, and 2 Monster Pulse.
  • The Garou world boss first spawns 30 minutes after server start, then every 30 minutes after the previous boss dies.
  • The tracked boss row also shows Cosmic Being at 0.525%, which this page treats as the direct style unlock entry from Garou.

Route notes

  • This route has two obtainment paths: the Punch Island NPC trade and the direct Garou boss unlock.
  • The Cosmic Being title is a real gate on the NPC route because it is part of the listed trade requirements.
  • Garou anchors the farming side of the page with a full HP and drop table instead of a placeholder boss section.

Cosmic Garou mechanics

  • Garou first spawns in the middle of Punch Island 30 minutes after server start and then respawns 30 minutes after the previous kill.
  • The timer only resets after the previous boss has been killed, so missed or stalled spawns should not be treated like a stacking timer.
  • Boss HP is not currently reported to scale upward with extra players attacking it.
  • At each 25% HP lost, the boss can trigger one of four adaptations: Harden for +10% extra damage reduction on top of its base 40%, Rage for +25% more damage against players, Accelerate for +25% movement speed, or Regenerate for 10% of lost HP.
  • That adaptation passive can only activate 3 times during one boss life, and the same adaptation can repeat again in a row.
  • Garou also appears to directly drop the Cosmic Being melee at a low chance, and the tracked boss row shows that line as Cosmic Being at 0.525%.
