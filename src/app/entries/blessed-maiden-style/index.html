Overview

Blessed Maiden

  • Blessed Maiden is a fighting style in Sailor Piece.
  • Blessed Maiden is known for balanced aoe with strong late-game scaling.
  • Blessed Maiden is unlocked through Blessed Maiden Trainer on Boss Island.
Section

How to obtain Blessed Maiden

  • Travel to Boss Island and head behind the two arches above the chessboard-like area to find the trainer.
  • Bring the Astral Empress title and the full material bundle along with 32,500 Gems.
