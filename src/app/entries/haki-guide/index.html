Overview

Haki

  • There are three Haki types in Sailor Piece: Armament, Observation, and Conqueror.
  • Armament Haki is your direct damage layer, Observation Haki is your dodge layer, and Conqueror Haki is your late-game passive plus active burst layer.
  • Armament Haki is consistently published at +25% base damage with +0.5% damage per level, while Observation Haki is consistently published at 5 starting dodges and +1 dodge every 10 levels up to 10 dodges.
  • Current checks support Conqueror Haki as both a passive damage buff and an active J-key blast that can also be set to auto-use in Settings. The safest current read is +1% damage per level, with the cap likely sitting somewhere around level 40 to 50.
  • Haki Color only changes the visual effect on Armament Haki and does not add stats.

What each Haki does

HakiEffectScaling and capHow to use
Armament HakiAdds a direct damage buff to your current build.Starts at +25% damage and gains +0.5% damage per level. The common settled ladder is about +62.5% at level 75, while newer endgame paths push it further if your server build supports higher levels.Press G to toggle it on and off.
Observation HakiAdds automatic dodges against incoming attacks.Starts with 5 dodges and gains about 1 extra dodge every 10 levels, reaching 10 dodges at level 50.Press H to activate the dodge pool.
Conqueror HakiAdds a passive damage increase and can also release a nearby AoE burst.Treat Conqueror as gaining +1% damage per level. The exact final cap is still not locked, but current player tracking points to it likely ending somewhere around level 40 or 50.Press J to trigger the Conqueror blast, or enable automatic use in Settings if your server build supports it.
