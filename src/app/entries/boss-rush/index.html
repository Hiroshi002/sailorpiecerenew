Overview

Boss Rush

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Boss Rush is started from Sailor Island by using 1 Rush Key at the central Boss Rush NPC.
  • Each run has 15 waves of random world bosses, a 300-second global timer, and can feature Escanor as the special boss.
  • You must deal at least 10% of the total run HP to qualify for the end rewards.
  • Rush Coins from the mode are used for both Boss Rush shop items and permanent upgrade stats.
Section

How to start Boss Rush

  • Travel to Sailor Island.
  • Go through the main gates and head to the right side of the island.
  • Find the three Boss Rush NPCs and use the central NPC to open the portal.
  • Spend 1 Rush Key and choose your difficulty before entering the 15-wave run.

Team play notes

  • Running with up to three players is still the safest clear-speed setup when the team can keep wave timers low.
  • You still need to personally contribute at least 10% of the total rush HP or the run will not pay out end rewards.
  • Extreme difficulty scales boss HP very hard and is much smoother with coordinated burst builds.
  • Escanor can appear up to three times in one run, but his spawn is not guaranteed.
