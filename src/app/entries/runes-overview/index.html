Overview

Runes

  • Runes are equipable passive items that boost damage, survivability, or luck.
  • The main rune pool comes from Rune Dungeons, while the two secret runes are tied to Infinite Tower.
  • Rune Dungeon drop chance scales with both difficulty and the later waves of a run, so W15 on Extreme is much better than early waves on Easy.
  • Primordial Rune and Radiant Rune sit at the top of current rune rankings, while Havoc Rune is the strongest non-secret damage rune in current tier lists.
  • Secret runes now also have an Infinite Tower pity tracker that adds +1 pity per cleared floor, with Radiant guaranteed at 7,500 floors and Primordial guaranteed at 10,000 floors.
Section

How runes work

  • Runes have their own inventory category; open your inventory, scroll right to the Runes section, and equip one rune at a time.
  • Most runes drop from Rune Dungeons, which have Easy, Medium, Hard, and Extreme difficulty settings and 15 waves each run.
  • Published rune chance notes usually use W1, W5, W10, and W15 as the shorthand checkpoints for each run.
  • Secret runes are separate from the normal dungeon pool and come from Infinite Tower instead, not Rune Dungeon.
Section

How to unlock Rune Dungeon

  • Get a Dungeon Key from world bosses such as Saber, Sukuna, Gojo, and other boss routes that drop keys.
  • Use a portal to reach Dungeon Island and talk to the Dungeon Master NPC.
  • Finish the unlock quest by collecting six Dungeon Puzzle Pieces and defeating 25 Bosses.
  • You need to be at least level 5,000 before you can start the Rune Dungeon route.

How to level up runes

  • Max Rune Level is 60.
  • Any rune you obtain adds +1 Rune EXP, no matter which rune type or rarity it is.
  • You need 700 total rune drops to reach max Rune Level.
  • Each Rune Level adds +1% potency, so Rune Level 60 gives a 1.6x multiplier to a rune's base effect.
  • Rune Level is shared across your rune collection, so your total rune drops decide how effective every equipped rune becomes.
  • This works more like artifact milestones than separate per-rune leveling: you are raising the global Rune Level, not feeding duplicates into one specific rune.

Secret rune notes

  • Radiant Rune is the main Infinite Tower chase rune for luck-focused farming builds.
  • Primordial Rune is the main Infinite Tower chase rune for pure damage and top-end DPS builds.
  • Both secret runes are tied to Infinite Tower rather than the normal Rune Dungeon pool.
  • Higher floors have much better published odds than low-floor farming, and the 2x Drop gamepass boosts both the base and max listed rates shown on the page.
  • The newest update also adds secret-rune pity at +1 per cleared Infinite Tower floor, with Radiant guaranteed at 7,500 total floors and Primordial guaranteed at 10,000 total floors.
Section

Best rune farming route

  • Farm world bosses for Dungeon Keys first, then spend the keys on Hard or Extreme Rune Dungeon runs because both higher difficulty and later waves raise the rune odds sharply.
  • If you are pushing a luck build or chasing the rarest drops, Infinite Tower is the route for Radiant Rune and Primordial Rune; higher floors and the 2x Drop gamepass both matter a lot more than low-floor resets.
  • Damage-focused players usually aim for Havoc, Wrath, or Primordial first, while farming builds usually prefer Fortune or Radiant.

Runes FAQ
How do you level up runes in Sailor Piece?
Rune Level is global, not per-rune. Any rune you obtain gives +1 Rune EXP, the cap is Rune Level 60, and you need 700 total rune drops to reach the 1.6x max potency multiplier.
Do secret runes come from Rune Dungeon?
No. Radiant Rune and Primordial Rune are Infinite Tower chase runes and are not in the normal Rune Dungeon pool.
What is the best non-secret rune right now?
For raw damage, Havoc Rune is the strongest non-secret pick on the current page. For farming and luck setups, Fortune Rune is the main non-secret route.
