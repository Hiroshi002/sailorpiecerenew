Section

How titles work

  • Open your inventory and switch to the Titles tab to equip a title.
  • Only one title should be treated as active at a time for your build.
  • Some late-game swords, melees, and routes ask for a title as a hard requirement, not just as a stat option.
  • Dragon Queen is still the top tracked sea-beast title on the page, and the latest update now puts it at 0.9% with pity 110.
  • Demon Dweller is now the highest raw-damage title tracked on the page at +100% Damage and is tied to Crystal Defense boss waves.

Titles FAQ
What do titles do in Sailor Piece?
Titles give stat bonuses such as damage, luck, HP, money, gems, or other progression boosts depending on the title line.
How do you get strong titles in Sailor Piece?
Most high-end titles come from bosses, special progression routes, Infinite Tower, or newer endgame update content.
Does the title page include boss titles and luck titles?
Yes. The page covers raw damage titles, luck titles, farming titles, event titles, and boss-related titles.
What is the best title in Sailor Piece?
That depends on the build. Demon Dweller is now the highest raw damage title tracked on the wiki, while Celestial Favor remains the main luck title route.
