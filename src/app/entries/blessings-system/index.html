Overview

Blessings System

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Blessings apply only to swords and fighting styles, not fruits or accessories.
  • Every sword and spec uses the same blessing ladder from B1 to B10.
  • A full B10 blessing adds +25% Damage, +5% Crit Chance, and +8% Crit Damage to that item.
Section

How to bless items

  • Go to Shibuya Station.
  • Head left from the portal to the Blessing NPC.
  • Choose the sword or spec you want to upgrade.
  • Bring the required materials for the next blessing level.

Material farming notes

  • Wood drops from any enemy and is the easiest blessing material to stock.
  • Iron starts dropping from Jungle Island and above.
  • Obsidian and Mythril come from Desert Island and higher areas.
  • Adamantite is the slowest blessing material, with published drops at Snow Island, Shibuya Station, and Hollow Island (Hueco Mundo) enemies.
