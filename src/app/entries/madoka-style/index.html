Overview

Madoka

  • Madoka is a fighting style in Sailor Piece.
  • Madoka is known for high burst with support-style abilities.
  • Madoka is unlocked through Madoka Trainer on Valentine Island.
Section

How to obtain Madoka

  • Travel to Valentine Island and go to the opposite end from the spawn point to find the trainer.
  • Bring the full bundle, including the King title and the large Heart requirement.
