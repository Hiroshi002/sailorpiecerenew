Overview

Stat Reroll NPC

  • The Stat Reroll NPC is on Sailor Island inside the same house as the Titles NPC, Fruit Dealers, and Storage NPC.
  • The current in-game reroll window shows seven rerollable stat lines: Damage, Defense, Cooldown Reduction, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Damage Reduction, and Luck.
  • Current public build guides push players toward SSS at minimum, while newer late-game advice also talks about chasing Z-tier lines once the rest of the build is already online.
  • Current reroll screenshots now confirm the full visible ladders for Damage, Defense, Cooldown Reduction, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, and Damage Reduction, while Luck still only has partially confirmed high-end values on this page.
  • Current rate screenshots confirm F 40%, E 25%, D 15%, C 10%, B 5%, A 3%, S 1.45%, SS 0.5%, SSS 0.05%, and a separate tracked Z chance of 0.0125% with no pity.
Section

Where to find the Stat Reroll NPC

  • Travel to Sailor Island.
  • Enter the same house that contains the Titles NPC, Storage NPC, Coin Fruit Dealer, and Gem Fruit Dealer.
  • The Stat Reroll NPC is part of that main Sailor Island utility hub, so it is one of the fastest systems to revisit while spending gems from codes or boss routes.

Pity and Z-tier notes

  • Reroll All costs 500 Gems in the current UI.
  • A single stat reroll costs 1,000 Gems in the current UI.
  • SS pity is tracked at 200 rerolls.
  • SSS pity is tracked at 500 rerolls.
  • Z has no pity and is being tracked separately at 0.0125%.

How players usually use this system

  • Damage, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, and Cooldown Reduction are usually the first lines players chase for combat builds.
  • Luck is more valuable when you are farming bosses, runes, artifact pieces, and low-drop materials.
  • Defense and Damage Reduction become more important for Infinite Tower, Boss Rush, and harder dungeon farming.
  • Do not burn all your gems here too early if you still need gems for key unlock systems like Haki, Powers, or late-game weapons.
