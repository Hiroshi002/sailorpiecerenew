Dungeon Pieces

Six hidden collectibles tied to the Dungeon Master questline that unlock the full dungeon system on Dungeon Island.

Systems Verified
Total pieces 6
Quest NPC Dungeon Master
Quest start Dungeon Island
Unlock reward Full dungeon access
Overview

Dungeon Pieces

  • Dungeon Pieces are part of the Dungeon Discovery questline from the Dungeon Master on Dungeon Island.
  • You need all 6 Dungeon Pieces plus 25 boss kills before dungeons unlock fully.
  • This route is the third major fixed collectible checklist in the wiki alongside Slime Puzzle and Hogyoku Fragments.
Section

How the Dungeon Piece route works

  • Reach level 5,000 and travel to Dungeon Island.
  • Talk to the Dungeon Master and start the Dungeon Discovery questline.
  • Collect all 6 hidden Dungeon Pieces, then finish the 25 boss kill requirement to unlock dungeons.

Section

Why players do this route

  • It unlocks Rune Dungeon, Shadow Dungeon, and Double Dungeon access.
  • The route is mandatory for later Shadow and Shadow Monarch progression lines.
  • It is a fixed collectible checklist, so it is much easier to finish than a pure low-drop grind once you know the locations.
