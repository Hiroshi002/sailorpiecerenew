Overview

Manipulator Sword (Aizen Sword)

  • Manipulator Sword, also called Aizen Sword, is obtained from the trainer NPC on Hollow Island (Hueco Mundo) after finishing the required quests.
  • The purchase bundle is 6,000,000 Money, 10,000 Gems, 10 Mirage Pendant, 6 Illusion Prism, 3 Reiatsu Core, and 1 Hogyoku Fragment.
  • Manipulator Sword uses M1, Z, X, C, and V and upgrades into True Aizen or True Manipulator through the Soul Dominion late-game path.
Section

How to obtain Manipulator Sword

  • Travel to Hollow Island (Hueco Mundo) and find the Manipulator Trainer on the back side of the island.
  • Complete the full trainer questline.
  • Return with the money, gems, and boss-drop material bundle.
