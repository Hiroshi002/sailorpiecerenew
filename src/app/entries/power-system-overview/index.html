Overview

Power System

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • The Power System unlocks at level 11,500 by paying 5,500 Gems to the Power Master on Lawless Island.
  • Unlike Spec Passives, powers buff your full build rather than only one sword or one fighting style.
  • The wiki currently tracks 18 powers, including the newer Sea 2 Mythical power Subjugator inside the current reroll pool notes.
  • Subjugator currently shows a slow effect that lowers hit NPC movement by 20% for 5 seconds and does not stack.
Section

How to unlock powers

  • Travel to Lawless Island.
  • Find the Power Master opposite the Arena Fighter NPCs.
  • Reach level 11,500 and bring 5,500 Gems.
  • Unlock the system once, then return with Power Shards to reroll.

Power System FAQ
What are powers in Sailor Piece?
Powers are late-game passive stat upgrades unlocked on Lawless Island that improve damage, luck, survivability, and full-build scaling.
When do you unlock powers in Sailor Piece?
Powers are unlocked later in progression once you can reach the Lawless Island route and meet the level gate for the system.
Do powers replace fruits or weapons?
No. Powers are a separate passive layer that boosts your full account on top of your fruit, weapon, melee, race, clan, and other systems.
What is the best power in Sailor Piece?
The strongest power depends on whether you want raw damage, farming luck, or a broad all-round build, but the page is ordered so the stronger overall picks are easier to spot.
