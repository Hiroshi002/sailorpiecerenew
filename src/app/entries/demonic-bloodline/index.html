Overview

Demonic Bloodline

  • Demonic is a newly confirmed bloodline from the current update wave.
  • The current tooltip shows Demonic granting +32% Damage, +12% Luck Multi, +2% Lifesteal, +12% Sword Damage Multi, and +40% Max HP.
  • The same tooltip also shows a 20% chance effect tied to +30% damage and a 10% slow, with a 5 second cooldown visible on the crop.
  • That stat package cleanly overtakes Astral as the strongest confirmed sword-focused bloodline on the site.
  • The exact rarity is still being checked live.
