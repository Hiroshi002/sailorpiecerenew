Overview

Races

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Races are rerolled from your inventory using Race Rerolls.
  • Higher-tier races can add large damage, HP, luck, lifesteal, or specific weapon bonuses to your build.
  • Certain swords care about specific races, with Swordblessed and Shinigami being two of the most important current examples.
Section

How to reroll races

  • Open your inventory.
  • Go to the Items tab and use a Race Reroll.
  • Confirm the reroll to replace your current race.
  • Save a stock of rerolls before chasing a late-game race because high-tier rolls are much rarer.
