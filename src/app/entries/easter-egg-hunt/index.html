Easter Egg Hunt

Sea 1 Easter quest guide with the official 7-egg structure, timed and boss egg spawn rules, and the confirmed completion rewards.

Overview

Easter Egg Hunt

  • The Easter Egg Hunt starts by talking to the Bunny NPC, then sending players across Sea 1 to find all 7 eggs.
  • Eggs 1 and 2 are timed eggs that spawn every 10 minutes at random spots around the map and despawn after 5 minutes.
  • Eggs 3, 4, and 5 are hidden eggs that stay on the map.
  • Eggs 6 and 7 are rare boss eggs with a 0.5% spawn chance every time any boss is killed, all players get notified when one appears, and each rare egg despawns after 5 minutes.
  • Completing the hunt rewards the Egg Seeker title, Egg Backpack accessory, and Petal Aura.
Section

How to run the Easter Egg Hunt

  • Talk to the Bunny NPC to start the quest.
  • Find all 7 eggs scattered around Sea 1.
  • Collect the 3 hidden eggs whenever you find them because they stay on the map.
  • Keep rotating for timed eggs because Eggs 1 and 2 only appear every 10 minutes and each one lasts 5 minutes.
  • Farm bosses while watching for the global rare-egg notification because Eggs 6 and 7 only have a 0.5% spawn chance when any boss dies and each rare egg also lasts 5 minutes.

Best way to finish the quest faster

  • Start the quest before doing any Sea 1 sweep so every egg pickup counts immediately.
  • Use Jungle Island, Snow Island, and Sailor Island as your fixed hidden-egg sweep because those are the clearest always-there spots across current guides.
  • For timed checks, prioritize Starter Island, Desert Island, Shibuya Station, Judgement Island, and Lawless Island before moving to weaker backup reports like Snow Island and Academy Island.
  • Grab the always-available hidden eggs first while the timed eggs are between spawns.
  • Do not camp one location too long because timed eggs and boss eggs can both disappear after 5 minutes.
  • If your server gets the rare-egg notification, stop what you are doing and search immediately because those boss eggs are the tightest part of the event.

Route notes that matter

  • The official quest structure is 2 timed eggs, 3 hidden eggs, and 2 boss eggs.
  • Boss eggs are tied to any boss kill, not one specific Easter boss route.
  • The rare-egg notification matters because every player in the server gets the alert when a boss egg spawns.
  • This quest is part of the same Easter Island event loop that feeds into Great Mage farming, so it is worth finishing even if you only care about the boss route.

Easter Egg Hunt FAQ
Where do you start the Easter Egg Hunt in Sailor Piece?
Start by talking to the Bunny NPC, which begins the 7-egg Sea 1 hunt.
How often do timed eggs spawn in the Easter Egg Hunt?
Eggs 1 and 2 spawn every 10 minutes at random spots around the map, and each timed egg despawns after 5 minutes.
What are the main egg locations players should check first?
The strongest always-there hidden egg spots are Jungle Island, Snow Island, and Sailor Island. The strongest timed checks from current guides are Starter Island, Desert Island, Shibuya Station, Judgement Island, and Lawless Island, with Snow Island and Academy Island as weaker backup reports.
How do the rare boss eggs appear?
Eggs 6 and 7 can appear after any boss kill with a 0.5% chance. When one spawns, all players get notified, and the egg despawns after 5 minutes.
What do you get for completing the Easter Egg Hunt?
Completing the hunt gives the Egg Seeker title, Egg Backpack accessory, and Petal Aura.
