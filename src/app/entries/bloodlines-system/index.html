Overview

Bloodlines

  • Bloodlines are one of the newly tracked Sea 2 systems.
  • Bloodline Stone is currently obtainable from Sea 2 NPCs and Sea Beasts.
  • The newer current bloodline table gives a cleaner exact stat read for the nine currently tracked bloodlines, so the page no longer needs the older cropped-screen guesswork.
  • Primordial is now the real top melee and luck-leaning bloodline on this page and replaces the older mistaken Cosmic placeholder.
  • Demonic is now the strongest confirmed sword-focused bloodline on the page, overtaking Astral with higher damage, sword damage multi, luck multi, max HP, and a newly visible proc line.
  • Primordial currently leads the confirmed melee-focused overall board, while Demonic leads the confirmed weapon-focused bloodline board.
Section

How bloodlines work right now

  • Bloodlines are a new Sea 2 system.
  • Bloodline Stones come from Sea 2 NPCs and Sea Beasts, with Sea Beasts acting as the strongest currently tracked route.
  • There is no separate Bloodline NPC right now because you use Bloodline Stones directly from your inventory.
  • The current bloodline table is now clean enough to lock in exact stats for the ten tracked entries on this page.
  • The older Cosmic entry has been removed because the corrected table shows Primordial instead.
  • The full bloodline list is now tracked on this page, with Legendary pity at 250 and Mythical pity at 750.

Current route notes

  • Primordial is now the real top-end melee and luck-leaning bloodline on the page and replaces the older mistaken Cosmic entry completely.
  • Demonic now takes over the sword bloodline lead from Astral because it cleanly upgrades every major visible sword stat line Astral was already using and also shows a stronger visible proc line on the newer tooltip.
  • Astral still holds a premium slot because it remains a very strong sword bloodline even after Demonic moves above it, especially now that its Mark effect is directly readable.
  • Dawnbringer and Nightfall remain strong mid-high options because both rows keep useful combat stats without wasting lines on weak filler bonuses.
  • Hunter, Vizard, and Stormborn are still useful depending on whether you care more about farming utility, gems, or XP support than pure combat caps.
  • This page should keep growing from direct tables and clean screenshots first, because those are much safer than rebuilding bloodlines from cropped menus.
