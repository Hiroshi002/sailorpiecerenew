Overview

Escanor

  • Escanor drops through Boss Rush on Sailor Island and the published drop rate scales with difficulty from 0.6% on Easy to 1.85% on Extreme.
  • The full Escanor-themed setup usually includes the Escanor weapon, Sun Armor from the Boss Rush Shop, the Pride clan, and the matching Escanor title line.
  • Boss Rush is also the only stable route for farming this weapon right now, so the sword and its armor naturally sit in the same progression loop.
Section

How to obtain Escanor

  • Travel to Sailor Island and enter Boss Rush by spending 1 Rush Key at the central Boss Rush NPC.
  • Clear the 15-wave run and watch for the Escanor Boss, who can appear up to three times in one run but is not guaranteed.
  • Higher difficulties improve the published weapon drop rate, so most serious farming starts at Hard or Extreme.
