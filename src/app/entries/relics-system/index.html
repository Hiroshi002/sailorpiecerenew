Overview

Relics

  • Relics are one of the newly tracked Sea 2 systems.
  • The Relic Crafter NPC is located on Bizarre Island in Sea 2.
  • The current Relic Forge screenshot confirms four current craftable relics: Luck Relic, Damage Relic, Crit Chance Relic, and Crit Damage Relic.
  • Relic Part routes are now tracked as #1 at 0.015% and #2 at 0.0125% from Delinquent, #3 at 0.01% and #4 at 0.009% from Strong Fighter, #5 at 0.008% and #6 at 0.007% from Strong Bandit, and #7 at 0.0075% plus #8 at 0.0065% from Fast Ninja.
  • Players searching Relic Part drop chance, Relic Part dropchance, or specific Relic Part #1 to #8 drop rates can use this page as the current Sea 2 reference.

How relic crafting works right now

  • Relics are a new Sea 2 system.
  • The Relic Crafter NPC is located on Bizarre Island in Sea 2.
  • Relic Part #1 and Relic Part #2 come from Delinquent on Starter Island Sea 2 at Level 12,750, currently tracked at 0.015% and 0.0125%.
  • Relic Part #3 and Relic Part #4 come from Strong Fighter on Starter Island Sea 2 at Level 13,000, currently tracked at 0.01% and 0.009%.
  • Relic Part #5 and Relic Part #6 come from Strong Bandit on Bizarre Island at Level 13,500, currently tracked at 0.008% and 0.007%.
  • Relic Part #7 and Relic Part #8 come from Fast Ninja on Punch Island at Level 14,500, currently tracked at 0.0075% and 0.0065%.
  • The current forge screenshot confirms recipes that use Relic Part #1 through Relic Part #8.
  • To equip Relics, scroll through your inventory near the accessories area.

Relics FAQ
What are the Relic Part drop chances in Sailor Piece?
The current Relic Part drop chances are #1 at 0.015% and #2 at 0.0125% from Delinquent, #3 at 0.01% and #4 at 0.009% from Strong Fighter, #5 at 0.008% and #6 at 0.007% from Strong Bandit, and #7 at 0.0075% plus #8 at 0.0065% from Fast Ninja.
Which NPC drops each Relic Part in Sailor Piece?
Delinquent drops Relic Part #1 and #2, Strong Fighter drops #3 and #4, Strong Bandit drops #5 and #6, and Fast Ninja drops #7 and #8 on the current Sea 2 routes.
