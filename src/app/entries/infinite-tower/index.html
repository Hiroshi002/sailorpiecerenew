Overview

Infinite Tower

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Infinite Tower is entered from Tower Island.
  • You need to be above level 8,500 and spend 1 Tower Key for each run.
  • Boss checkpoints appear every 5 floors, and debuffs stack harder after floor 50 and floor 100.
  • Radiant Rune and Primordial Rune are the two secret chase runes currently tied to Infinite Tower.
  • Secret runes now also have a pity tracker at +1 per cleared floor, with Radiant guaranteed at 7,500 floors and Primordial guaranteed at 10,000 floors.
  • Beebom's March 28, 2026 guide says every cleared level in Infinite Tower gives Tower Tokens, and the amount depends on how far the run goes.
  • The same guide says Infinite Tower runs also reward money, XP, gems, Race Rerolls, Trait Rerolls, Clan Rerolls, and a low chance at Tower Shop items directly from the mode.
  • Fully maxing the permanent Infinite Tower upgrade tree costs 36,500 Tower Tokens.
  • Newer Sea 2 player notes also tie a Ragna Tower Mode path in Infinite Tower to one side of the Sea 2 map-piece route, but that route is currently reported bugged and can restart the quest.
Section

How to enter Infinite Tower

  • Travel to Tower Island through a portal.
  • Make sure you are above level 8,500.
  • Bring a Tower Key and talk to the Infinite Tower NPC.
  • Spawn the portal and enter to begin the run.

Current Ragna tower warning

  • Current checks suggest Tower Mode is the Ragna side of one Sea 2 map-piece route.
  • That same route is currently reported bugged, and joining it can restart the quest progress instead of helping the map-piece line.
  • If you only need the map piece right now, Vampire King (Alucard) is the safer current fallback and the map pieces do not need to be completed in strict order.
Section

How to grind Infinite Tower

  • Use a damage build, not a luck build, when you are grinding Infinite Tower because faster clears and deeper floors matter more than squeezing out extra luck if you want more floor rewards, better rune odds, and a steadier reroll loop.
  • The easiest AFK setup is a large AoE autocast move that can keep clearing waves by itself, with Gilgamesh V move being one of the clearest examples if your account has it.
  • First, go into the tower settings and set auto-reset around floor 5 to 10 so the run loops back to floor 0 and starts over by itself while you test your setup.
  • After the loop looks stable, tick the auto-restart box too. Auto-restart is the retry prompt you get after dying, where the game asks if you want to start the tower again from floor 1 instead of kicking you out.
  • Once both settings are working correctly, raise the auto-reset target back up to around floor 50 to 70 depending on how strong your build is and let the tower keep running on its own.
  • If the run slows down or starts dying before the target floor, lower the reset point again until the loop is consistent instead of forcing a weaker luck setup.

Secret rune notes

  • Radiant Rune is the main Infinite Tower chase rune for luck-focused farming builds.
  • Primordial Rune is the main Infinite Tower chase rune for pure damage and top-end DPS builds.
  • Both secret runes are tied to Infinite Tower rather than the normal Rune Dungeon pool.
  • Higher floors have much better published odds than low-floor farming, and the 2x Drop gamepass boosts both the base and max listed rates shown on the page.
  • The newest update also adds +1 secret-rune pity per cleared floor, with Radiant guaranteed at 7,500 total floors and Primordial guaranteed at 10,000 total floors.

Best published rewards

  • Abyssal Empress is one of the main reasons players grind this mode.
  • Abyssal Outfit and Purple Flare Aura are the most visible shop-exclusive cosmetics tied to the tower grind.
  • The mode also pays money, XP, gems, rerolls, and a low chance at direct shop items from the tower itself.
  • Permanent Infinite Tower upgrades now have screenshot-confirmed token ladders for Damage, Critical Damage, Critical Chance, HP, and Luck, so you can plan the full 36,500-token investment more accurately.
