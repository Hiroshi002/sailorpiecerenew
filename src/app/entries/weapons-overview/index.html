Overview

Weapons and Swords

  • This wiki currently publishes 18 dedicated sword pages.
  • The direct upgrade chains in the sword category are Shadow into Atomic, Jinwoo into Shadow Monarch, and Manipulator Sword into True Aizen or True Manipulator.
  • Excalibur, sometimes written as Saber Sword, stays a sword route, while Corrupted Excalibur or Saber Alter belongs to a separate fighting style path.
  • Anti Magic is now tracked as the newest sword page, with Asta as the main alias and both the Crystal Defense drop route and 5,000 Crystal Coin shop route now confirmed.
Section

Sword progression path

  • Katana, Dark Blade, Gryphon, and Excalibur are the early or midgame bridge swords.
  • Ichigo and Rimuru are full boss-material swords that sit in the middle of the late-game grind before the top update swords.
  • Ragna sits in the older dragon-sword lane and is often treated as a collector or trade route when its boss line is unavailable.
  • Anti Magic is the newest sword page on the site and is now confirmed through the Crystal Defense boss loop plus the 5,000 Crystal Coin shop route while still being tracked under both the Anti Magic and Asta search names.
  • Shadow, Jinwoo, and Aizen Sword are the base weapons for the big endgame upgrade chains.
  • Escanor, Atomic, Shadow Monarch, True Aizen, Abyssal Empress, Yamato, and Ice Queen are the major late-game sword goals right now.
  • Corrupted Excalibur or Saber Alter belongs to the spec side of the wiki, not to the sword evolution table.

Current Sea 2 sword notes

  • Anti Magic is now the newest featured sword page, and the current confirmed route is Crystal Defense at the back of Punch Island with boss waves every 5 rounds plus a 5,000 Crystal Coin shop fallback.
  • Ice Queen still holds the broad overall lead on the sword side because it stays strong across almost every PvE lane instead of only one niche.
  • Great Mage moves up into S+ because the kit lands much better in practice than it first looks, thanks to very fast moves, great damage, and low cooldowns.
  • Dragon Goddess moves back into S+ because the latest reported buff wave is enough to treat it as a top-end Sea 2 sword again instead of only a balanced middle-ground option.
