Overview

Jinwoo

  • Jinwoo is bought from the Jinwoo Trainer on Sailor Island after completing the Jinwoo questline.
  • The purchase bundle is 2,500,000 Money, 7,500 Gems, 6 Abyss Edge, 3 Dark Ring, and 1 Shadow Heart.
  • Jinwoo uses M1, Z, X, and C and later acts as the base weapon for the Shadow Monarch evolution path.
Section

How to obtain Jinwoo

  • Travel to Sailor Island and talk to the Jinwoo Trainer.
  • Finish the five trainer quests.
  • Return with 2,500,000 Money, 7,500 Gems, 6 Abyss Edge, 3 Dark Ring, and 1 Shadow Heart.
