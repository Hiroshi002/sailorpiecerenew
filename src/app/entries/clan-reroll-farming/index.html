Overview

Best Clan Reroll Farm

  • Clan Rerolls are the item used to reroll clans and also act as the shared CCR trading anchor on this wiki.
  • Beebom's March 28, 2026 Infinite Tower guide says the mode directly rewards Clan Rerolls and also gives Tower Tokens every level, with the token payout depending on how far the run goes.
  • Sea Serpent and Kraken are the strongest currently tracked burst-payout routes, with the current FAQ sheet showing Clan Reroll at 100% with a quantity of 25 on each kill.
  • Fast Ninja is the best published normal-enemy Clan Reroll farm on this wiki at 0.58505%, with Strong Bandit right behind it at 0.56505% and Arena Fighter now acting as the best older Sea 1 fallback at 0.5151%.
Section

Quick answer

  • Current wiki recommendation: Infinite Tower is the best stable Clan Reroll farm if your build can loop it consistently, because published guides confirm the mode directly rewards Clan Rerolls and also gives Tower Tokens every cleared level, with better payout the farther a run goes.
  • Sea Serpent and Kraken are the best burst-payout routes currently tracked on this site, because the current FAQ sheet shows Clan Reroll at 100% with a quantity of 25 on each kill while also overlapping Bloodline Stone, Dragon Goddess, Blossom Outfit, and Kraken Armor.
  • If you want a pure normal-enemy fallback, Fast Ninja is the best published Clan Reroll mob line at 0.58505%, with Strong Bandit next at 0.56505% and Arena Fighter still acting as the best older Sea 1 fallback at 0.5151%.
  • Codes are still the best instant free spike when they are active, but they are not a repeatable farming method.

Best Clan Reroll Farm FAQ
What is the best current way to farm Clan Rerolls in Sailor Piece?
The current wiki recommendation is Infinite Tower for the best stable route, Kraken for the best burst payout, and Fast Ninja for the best normal-enemy fallback.
Is Kraken better than Infinite Tower for Clan Rerolls?
Sea Serpent and Kraken currently look better for burst payout per kill because the current FAQ sheet shows a guaranteed 25 Clan Rerolls on each kill, but Infinite Tower is still the cleaner stable route for long looping because the mode itself rewards rerolls and scales tokens by depth.
What is the best normal enemy for Clan Rerolls?
Fast Ninja is the best published normal-enemy Clan Reroll route on this wiki at 0.58505%, with Strong Bandit next at 0.56505% and Arena Fighter still acting as the best older Sea 1 fallback at 0.5151%.
Do other sites agree on the best Clan Reroll farm?
Not fully. Most public guides still list broad source categories like NPCs, bosses, dungeons, chests, merchants, or shop routes, but they usually do not publish the same route-specific ranking used on this page.
