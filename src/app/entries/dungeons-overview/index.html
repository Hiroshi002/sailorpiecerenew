Dungeons

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Dungeons unlock at level 5,000 through the Dungeon Master questline on Dungeon Island.
  • You must collect six Dungeon Pieces and defeat 25 bosses before the dungeon menu unlocks.
  • The current dungeon types are Shadow Dungeon, Rune Dungeon, and Double Dungeon.
Section

How to unlock dungeons

  • Reach level 5,000 and travel to Dungeon Island.
  • Talk to the Dungeon Master NPC and accept the Dungeon Discovery quest.
  • Collect all 6 Dungeon Pieces across the world.
  • Defeat any 25 bosses to finish the second half of the questline.
  • After that, each dungeon run still consumes 1 Dungeon Key.

Section

Difficulty rules

  • Each dungeon has Easy, Medium, Hard, and Extreme modes.
  • Every run has 15 waves.
  • If you die or disconnect, the Dungeon Key is still consumed.
  • Rune Dungeon gets much better rune value at higher difficulty, but enemy HP scales heavily.
