Overview

Auras

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Auras are separate from cosmetics and mainly serve as damage-boosting visual effects.
  • Most current auras come from Aura Crates, while some older event auras are no longer obtainable.
  • Aura damage buffs are tracked as a flat damage increase based on rarity.
  • A current live inventory capture now confirms current owned aura names such as Fallen Aura, Saiyyan Aura, Winged Aura, Commander Aura, Corrupted Aura, Glacier Aura, Psycho Aura, Tempest Aura, Ultra Instinct Aura, Gambler Aura, Singer Aura, Rose Aura, and Alter Aura.
  • Destroyer Aura is now the directly confirmed new Crystal Defense aura, sold by the Defense Merchant for 2,000 Crystal Coins and confirmed as Legendary.
  • Demonic Aura is now also confirmed as a real Legendary aura from the current update wave through the Discord FAQ.

How to get aura crates

  • Aura Crates can drop from mobs, mini-bosses, and world bosses.
  • Some current codes also give Aura Crates.
  • Published guides also point to token shop routes for crate farming during recent updates.
  • Crystal Defense also now has a direct Defense Merchant shop route for Destroyer Aura at 2,000 Crystal Coins.
  • Demonic Aura is now confirmed as Legendary from the Discord FAQ, but its exact obtainment route still needs a direct in-game capture if it differs from the normal aura pool.
Section

How to equip auras

  • Open inventory and switch to the Aura tab.
  • Equip the aura you want to use.
  • Only one aura should be treated as active on your build at a time.
