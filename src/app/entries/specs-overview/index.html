Overview

Melees and Fighting Styles

  • There are currently 17 fighting styles in Sailor Piece if you count Combat, which is the default melee style.
  • Fighting styles are unlocked from trainer NPCs, boss exchange routes, material bundles, and title, race, or clan gates.
  • Spec Passives are a separate Judgement Island system that can be applied onto a fighting style after you unlock it.

How to read the melee tiers

  • S+ styles are the strongest current endgame picks and usually define the meta for burst, farming, or both.
  • The World and Cosmic Being currently sit together in S+ on this wiki: The World still owns the AFK and repeat-loop niche, while the latest reported Garou buff wave is enough to push Cosmic Being back into the top live-damage lane too.
  • S tier styles are still elite and often come down to whether you want easier farming, better bossing, or specific build synergy.
  • A and B tier styles are still fully usable, but they fall behind the top styles once you move deeper into late-game content.
  • Combat stays at the bottom only because it is the starter style, not because it is unusable in the early game.
