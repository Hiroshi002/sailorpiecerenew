Overview

Ichigo

  • Ichigo can be obtained either by buying it for 500 Boss Tickets or by purchasing it from the Ichigo Seller on Hollow Island with boss materials.
  • The crafting route uses 15,000 Gems, 8 Soul Fragments, 4 Spiritual Cores, 2 Soul Flames, and the Soul Reaper title.
  • Ichigo is one of the cleaner late-game swords because the boss route, title, accessory, and core materials all sit around the same Ichigo grind.
Section

How to obtain Ichigo

  • Travel to Hollow Island and find the Ichigo Seller if you want the full boss-material route.
  • Bring 15,000 Gems, 8 Soul Fragments, 4 Spiritual Cores, 2 Soul Flames, and the Soul Reaper title.
  • If you only want the sword faster, you can also buy Ichigo for 500 Boss Tickets through the Boss Exchange route.

Where to get the required materials

  • Soul Fragments, Spiritual Cores, Soul Flames, Hollow Mask, and the Soul Reaper title all come from the Ichigo Boss route.
  • Boss Tickets can be farmed from bosses across the world and spent at Boss Exchange if you want the shortcut purchase.
  • Hollow Island is the key island to know for the seller route, while Boss Island covers the summon route.
