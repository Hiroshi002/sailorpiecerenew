Overview

Saber Alter

  • Saber Alter is a fighting style in Sailor Piece.
  • Saber Alter is known for high burst melee with corruption-based abilities.
  • Saber Alter is unlocked through Saber Alter Trainer on Boss Island.
Section

How to obtain Saber Alter

  • Travel to Boss Island and find the Saber Alter Trainer on the left side near the teleport side of the island.
  • Bring the Corrupt Tyrant title and the full corruption material bundle to unlock the style.
