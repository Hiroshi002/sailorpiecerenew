Overview

Moon Slayer

  • Moon Slayer is a fighting style in Sailor Piece.
  • Moon Slayer is known for quick abilities with low cooldown and burst damage.
  • Moon Slayer is unlocked through Moon Slayer Trainer on Boss Island.
Section

How to obtain Moon Slayer

  • Travel to Boss Island and go behind the boss arena to find the Moon Slayer NPC on the left-side rock.
  • Bring the Six Eyed Demon title, 37,500 Gems, and the full moon material bundle.
