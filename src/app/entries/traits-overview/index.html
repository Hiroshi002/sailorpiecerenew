Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • There are 25 current traits spread across Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythical, and Secret rarities.
  • Traits are rerolled with Trait Rerolls at the Trait NPC on Sailor Island.
  • Current public trait lists agree that Emperor, Celestial, Singularity, Cataclysm, and Overlord sit at the top end of the meta.
Section

How to reroll traits

  • Farm or redeem Trait Rerolls.
  • Go to Sailor Island and talk to the Trait NPC.
  • Use one Trait Reroll per spin.
  • Bosses have the highest Trait Reroll drop rate, while low-level mobs have the weakest drop rate.

Traits FAQ
What do traits do in Sailor Piece?
Traits add strong passive bonuses such as damage, cooldown reduction, crit support, and other combat scaling that affect almost every build.
Does the traits page show every trait and its stats?
Yes. The traits page is meant to list all tracked traits with their stat lines, not just the best few.
How do you reroll traits in Sailor Piece?
Traits are rerolled with Trait Rerolls, which are available through gameplay rewards, codes, and store products.
What is the best trait in Sailor Piece?
The exact best trait can shift with balance and build type, but Emperor and other top-end traits are clearly separated from the weaker reroll options.
