Overview

Yamato

  • Yamato is bought from the Yamato Seller on Judgement Island.
  • The purchase bundle is 30,000 Gems, 14 Frozen Will, 7 Yamato Essence, 3 Silent Storm, 1 Azure Heart, the Swordblessed race, and the Blade Sovereign title.
  • Yamato has a built-in five-skill move set with Z, X, C, V, and F and does not use a separate F-move mastery route in the checked guides.
Section

How to obtain Yamato

  • Travel to Judgement Island and climb to the Yamato Seller.
  • Make sure you have the Swordblessed race and the Blade Sovereign title.
  • Bring 30,000 Gems, 14 Frozen Will, 7 Yamato Essence, 3 Silent Storm, and 1 Azure Heart.

Where to get the required materials

  • Frozen Will, Yamato Essence, Silent Storm, Azure Heart, and the Blade Sovereign title come from the Yamato Boss on Judgement Island.
  • Swordblessed must be rolled separately through race rerolls.
  • Gems come from quests, chests, bosses, and codes.
