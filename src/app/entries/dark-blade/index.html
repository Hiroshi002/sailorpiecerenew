Overview

Dark Blade

  • Dark Blade is bought from the Dark Blade Seller on Snow Island.
  • The purchase cost is 250,000 Money and 150 Gems.
  • Dark Blade uses an early two-skill layout with Z and X and does not need a questline, mastery route, or evolution.
Section

How to obtain Dark Blade

  • Travel to Snow Island.
  • Find the Dark Blade Seller near the teleport side of the island.
  • Buy Dark Blade for 250,000 Money and 150 Gems.
