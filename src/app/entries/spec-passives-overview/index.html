Spec Passives

Judgement Island passive system that adds a rerolled buff layer onto your swords and fighting styles.

Overview

Spec Passives

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Spec Passives are a separate late-game system and are not the melee category itself.
  • The system unlocks at Judgement Island after reaching level 10,000 and paying 5,000 Gems.
  • Once unlocked, you can apply rerolled passives onto swords and fighting styles using Passive Shards.
Section

How to unlock spec passives

  • Use a portal to reach Judgement Island.
  • Go straight from the portal and find the six-eyed samurai NPC on the right.
  • Pay 5,000 Gems once you are level 10,000 or higher.
  • After that, return to the same NPC to reroll passives with Passive Shards.
