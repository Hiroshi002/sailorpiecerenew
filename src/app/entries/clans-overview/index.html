Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • The wiki currently tracks 14 named clans after the Crystal Defense update, with Devil as the newest clan.

Section

How to reroll clans

  • Open your inventory and go to the Items tab.
  • Use a Clan Reroll to replace your current clan.
  • Clan Rerolls come from routes like Infinite Tower floor drops, bosses, dungeons, chests, codes, and a few merchant or shop fallbacks.
  • Save a pile of rerolls before you chase the top legendary clans because the late-game pool is much more crowded now.

Clans FAQ
What do clans do in Sailor Piece?
Clans give passive stat boosts and often define whether a build leans into swords, melees, luck farming, or special late-game weapon routes.
How do you change clans in Sailor Piece?
Clans are rerolled with Clan Rerolls, which is also why players use CCR as a trading currency shortcut.
Are the strongest clans listed near the bottom or top?
The stronger and rarer clans are pushed lower in the page order so players can scan toward the strongest end of the list faster.
Which clans matter most for endgame builds?
Top builds now revolve around clans like Devil, Upper, Eminence, Pride, and Frostbane depending on whether you want sword damage, melee damage, or farming value.
