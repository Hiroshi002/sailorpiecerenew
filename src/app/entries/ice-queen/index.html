Ice Queen

Boss Island late-game sword built around the Ice Queen boss, Frost Empress title, and a full bundle of frost materials.

Overview

Ice Queen

  • Ice Queen is bought from the Ice Queen Seller on Boss Island for 40,000 Gems plus a boss-material bundle and the Frost Empress title.
  • The base unlock needs 1 Ice Core, 4 Frozen Brand, 9 Glacier Remnant, 17 Battle Shard, 25 Frost Relic, and the Frost Empress title.
  • Ice Queen uses Z, X, C, V, and a mastery-locked F move that requires Frostbane clan and extra Ice Queen materials.
Section

How to obtain Ice Queen

  • Travel to Boss Island and find the Ice Queen Seller under the hand statue area on the right side.
  • Farm the Ice Queen boss for the boss-only drops and title, while stacking Frost Relics from general enemy farming.
  • Bring the materials and 40,000 Gems to the seller NPC.
