Overview

Bosses

  • Current major guides break Sailor Piece bosses into open-world bosses, spawnable bosses, dungeon or tower bosses, and unavailable event bosses.
  • Most Boss Island summons are started through the Boss Summoner NPC and consume Boss Keys, money, gems, or special summoning items.
  • Boss fights are one of the main sources of Boss Tickets, Dungeon Keys, titles, accessories, and many of the weapon and melee materials used across the wiki.
  • The best boss pages are the ones that also tell you what the drops are actually for, because summon items, titles, weapons, melees, and materials all chain into each other.
  • World Boss now has an in-game countdown timer, so players no longer have to rely on pure guesswork between spawns.
  • The same live balance wave also says world-boss and sea-beast drop chances were buffed, though the exact new percentages still need cleaner post-buff captures.

Section

Best published farming loop

  • Shibuya Station for Yuji, Gojo, and Sukuna is one of the best reroll and key loops.
  • Sailor Island for Jinwoo and Alucard is still one of the easiest mixed boss-ticket and material routes.
  • Hollow Island for Aizen is one of the better late-game reroll and fragment routes.
  • Boss Island becomes the main focus once you need Boss Tickets, summon materials, or title-locked weapon and melee routes.
  • Lawless Island, Ninja Island, Judgement Island, and Slime Island become the important loop once you are pushing Atomic, Strongest Shinobi, Yamato, or Rimuru.
