Normal Enemy Drop Charts

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • This page focuses on normal-enemy drop tables, especially the utility items players ask about most: Race Rerolls, Trait Rerolls, Clan Rerolls, Haki Color Rerolls, chests, keys, crates, shard drops, and the newer Sea 2 material lines.
  • Fast Ninja currently leads most published normal-enemy chest, crate, key, shard, and Clan Reroll lines on the tracked sheet, while Curse still holds the strongest Haki Color Reroll and Trait Reroll rows.
  • A few lines in the later source-sheet rows duplicate one utility label or crop awkwardly, so the item charts keep the clearly readable values and leave the messier rows explained in the snapshot notes.
  • If you are searching for rare NPC material drop chances, the late Sea 2 rows on this page are the current published Guild Key, Dominion Brand, Bloodline Stone, Upper Seal, Power Shard, and Relic Part normal-enemy sources.
Section

How to use these charts

  • Use the item charts first if you want to compare one item across multiple enemies, especially rerolls, chests, keys, and utility currencies.
  • Use the snapshot tables lower on the page when you want to pick one enemy and see the broader farming overlap around it.
  • If a row in the original source sheet duplicated a label or cropped awkwardly, the chart keeps the confirmed values and the snapshot note calls out the messy part instead of pretending it was clean.

Normal Enemy Drop Charts FAQ
Where should you farm Race Rerolls from normal enemies in Sailor Piece?
On the current normal-enemy sheet, Arena Fighter shares the top published Race Reroll line at 1.3% with Delinquent, Strong Fighter, Strong Bandit, and Fast Ninja, while Ninja, Strong Sorcerer, Hollow, and Curse still sit in the next best late-game band.
Which normal enemy has the best chest rates right now?
Fast Ninja currently leads the clearly published normal-enemy chest rows, topping Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythical, Aura Crate, and Cosmetic Crate rates on this page.
Why do some late normal-enemy rows mention a duplicate or cropped source line?
A few of the later screenshot rows repeat one utility label or crop a label awkwardly. The page keeps the clearly readable values in the charts and calls out the messy lines in the notes instead of treating them as settled.
Which NPC has the best Guild Key drop chance from normal enemies?
Fast Ninja currently leads the published normal-enemy Guild Key line at 0.00026342%, with Strong Bandit next at 0.00024742%, Strong Fighter at 0.00019742%, and Delinquent at 0.00019342%.
Which NPC drops Dominion Brand in Sailor Piece?
Strong Bandit [Lv.13500] on Bizarre Island is the current published normal-enemy Dominion Brand source on this wiki, with a tracked drop chance of 0.1925%.
Which NPCs drop Relic Parts in Sailor Piece?
Delinquent drops Relic Part #1 and #2, Strong Fighter drops #3 and #4, Strong Bandit drops #5 and #6, and Fast Ninja drops #7 and #8 on the current Sea 2 normal-enemy sheet.
