Overview

Shadow Monarch

  • Shadow Monarch is bought from the Shadow Monarch NPC on Dungeon Island.
  • The base unlock needs the Jinwoo sword, also commonly searched as Solo Hunter, plus the King of Shadows title and four Shadow Monarch materials.
  • Shadow Monarch has a mastery-locked F move that adds a permanent 30% damage buff on top of the unlock.
Section

How to obtain Shadow Monarch

  • Own the Jinwoo sword first.
  • Travel to Dungeon Island and climb to the roof of the dungeon structure.
  • Bring the King of Shadows title and the full Shadow Monarch boss material bundle.
