Overview

Accessories

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Accessories are separate from cosmetics and auras and usually come from bosses, shops, or special quest rewards.
  • Most strong accessories add a mix of defense, damage, and damage reduction.
  • Late-game accessories matter even more because they can be enchanted at Shibuya Station.
  • Crystal Defense now adds Demon Wing as a boss-drop accessory and Clover Outfit as a Crystal Coin shop accessory on Punch Island.
Section

How accessories work

  • Accessories sit in their own inventory tab and are separate from cosmetics, auras, and artifacts.
  • Most current accessories come from boss drops, but some newer ones come from Boss Rush, Infinite Tower, merchants, or quest rewards.
  • Once you unlock a strong piece, upgrade it through the Enchanter NPC at Shibuya Station to push it further.

Accessories FAQ
What do accessories do in Sailor Piece?
Accessories add major offensive and defensive stats and become much stronger when enchanted from E0 to E10.
Does the accessories page compare different enchant levels?
Yes. The accessories page includes comparison support so players can compare pieces at E0 through E10.
What is the best accessory in Sailor Piece?
The best accessory depends on the current build path, but Demon Wing now leads the strongest confirmed broad stat line on the site, while Clover Outfit gives the best guaranteed shop route and Kraken Armor, Cosmic Body, Blossom Outfit, World Outfit, Mage Outfit, Ice Queen Outfit, and other late-game outfits still define the rest of the endgame accessory pool.
Can I compare only a few accessories instead of the whole list?
Yes. The accessory page includes a selector so you can filter the table down to the pieces you actually want to compare.
