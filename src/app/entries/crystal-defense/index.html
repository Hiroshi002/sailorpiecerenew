Crystal Defense

Punch Island survival mode at the back of Sea 2 where you spend 1 Crystal Key to defend a crystal, push through boss waves every 5 rounds, and farm Crystal Coins for the new shop and upgrades.

Overview

Crystal Defense

  • Crystal Defense is located at the back of Punch Island in Sea 2.
  • The mode costs 1 Crystal Key to enter and asks you to protect the crystal from enemy waves.
  • A random boss spawns every 5 waves, and that boss can drop both Anti Magic and Demon Wing.
  • Using the shown 819.8% luck check as the conversion anchor, the published boss checks normalize to about 0.048% to 0.064% for Anti Magic and about 0.060% to 0.080% for Demon Wing on a 100%-luck baseline.
  • Current testing suggests the cleanest AFK route here uses Cosmic Being V because it has no end lag and can recast instantly when cooldown finishes.
  • Current checks place the best Cosmic Being V AFK spots either in the middle of the lane or directly inside the crystal.
  • Crystal Defense also has a leaderboard, a Defense Merchant shop, and a separate Defense Upgrades screen.
  • Current update checks also tie Devil clan, Demonic bloodline, and Demon Dweller title to Crystal Defense boss waves.
  • Current testing also suggests +1 drop effects do not increase Crystal Coin payouts, the same way they do not increase Boss Rush currency.
Section

How to start Crystal Defense

  • Travel to the back of Punch Island in Sea 2.
  • Bring a Crystal Key before you try to start the mode.
  • Crystal Key is currently confirmed as dropping from any NPC in Sea 2.
  • Enter the mode, defend the crystal, and keep pushing through waves for as long as possible.
  • Be ready for a random boss to spawn every 5 waves.

Best AFK farm setup right now

  • Current testing suggests Cosmic Being V is the cleanest AFK option for Crystal Defense because the move has no end lag and fires again as soon as cooldown finishes.
  • Set up your AFK loop from either the middle of the lane or directly inside the crystal, since those are the two positions current players are reporting as the most stable.
  • This is a mode-specific AFK note for Crystal Defense and is mainly about keeping wave clear speed steady while the crystal stays protected.

Section

Defense upgrade notes

  • Each upgrade line now has a fully confirmed 5-level ladder from live in-game checks.
  • Damage, Crit Damage, and Crit Chance each cost 8,500 Crystal Coins total to max.
  • HP costs 5,350 Crystal Coins total to max, while Luck costs 12,500 Crystal Coins total to max.
  • Fully maxing all five Crystal Defense permanent upgrades costs 43,350 Crystal Coins.
  • Current testing suggests +1 drop effects do not raise Crystal Coin payouts, so extra-drop farming layers should not be counted as part of the coin grind here.
  • Current new-server checks point to about 3 Crystal Coins on lower waves, with the payout rising at higher waves.
