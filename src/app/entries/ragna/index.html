Overview

Ragna

  • Ragna is tied to the Dragon Slayer route and is associated with Snow Island seller and mastery NPCs.
  • The sword's main material line is tied to the Ragna boss, which newer trackers often mark as currently unavailable.
  • Ragna has an F move route through the Dragon Slayer Master on Snow Island.
Section

How to obtain Ragna

  • Travel to Snow Island and look for the Dragon Slayer Seller route.
  • Older weapon and NPC lists track Ragna as a sword tied to the Snow Island dragon route.
  • Newer boss trackers often mark the Ragna boss as currently unavailable, so the sword may only be obtainable when that route is live again or through player trading.
