Overview

Strongest of Today

  • Strongest of Today is a fighting style in Sailor Piece.
  • Strongest of Today is known for high aoe damage with strong farming potential.
  • Strongest of Today is unlocked through Strongest of Today Trainer on Shinjuku Island.
Section

How to obtain Strongest of Today

  • Travel to Shinjuku Island and climb to the last building in the left corner to find the trainer.
  • Consume 6 Six Eyes first, then bring the listed materials and the Strongest Sorcerer title.
