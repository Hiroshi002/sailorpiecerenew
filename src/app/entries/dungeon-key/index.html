Overview

Dungeon Key

  • Dungeon Key is the entry item consumed for dungeon runs after you unlock dungeons.
  • The current Dungeons page highlights Aizen at 30% as one of the best tracked boss routes, with Sukuna, Jinwoo, and Ichigo at 20%, Gojo and Qin Shi at 15%, and Saber at 10%.
  • On the normal-enemy sheet, Fast Ninja currently leads Dungeon Key at 0.95%, with Strong Bandit at 0.9% and Delinquent plus Strong Fighter at 0.88%.
Section

How to get Dungeon Key

  • Farm strong bosses if you want the biggest per-kill Dungeon Key payouts.
  • Open higher chests if you are stacking chest routes at the same time.
  • Use late normal enemies like Fast Ninja if you want a steady background Dungeon Key farm while chasing other materials.

What Dungeon Key is used for

  • Dungeon Key is consumed to enter dungeons after the Dungeon Discovery unlock.
  • That includes Shadow Dungeon, Rune Dungeon, and Double Dungeon.
  • Once you have the unlock quest finished, the real bottleneck becomes how fast you can keep replacing used keys.
