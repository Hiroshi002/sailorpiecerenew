Skill Tree

Slime Island stat tree that unlocks at level 7,000 and uses Skill Points earned by defeating NPCs.

Systems Verified
Unlock level 7,000
Unlock cost 1,000 Gems
Location Slime Island

Overview

Skill Tree

  • The Skill Tree unlocks on Slime Island at level 7,000 by paying 1,000 Gems to the Skill Tree NPC.
  • Players earn 1 Skill Point for every 250 NPCs defeated, so mob farming is much better than boss farming for this system.
  • The current Skill Tree has five branches: Luck, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, HP%, and Damage%.
Section

How to unlock the Skill Tree

  • Reach level 7,000 or higher.
  • Travel to Slime Island and head to the small huts on the right side.
  • Talk to the Skill Tree NPC and pay 1,000 Gems to unlock the system.
  • Once unlocked, you can spend Skill Points in order across the five branches.

Section

Best early upgrade order

  • Damage % is one of the best first branches because it boosts your whole build immediately.
  • Crit Damage and Crit Chance are the next best combat branches once your base damage is online.
  • Luck matters a lot for long farming sessions, but it is also the most expensive branch to max.
  • HP % is still worth finishing later because late-game survivability checks get much harsher in Tower, Boss Rush, and harder dungeons.
