Overview

Demon Wing

  • Demon Wing is one of the newly confirmed Crystal Defense rewards.
  • Demon Wing drops from the random boss that appears every 5 waves in Crystal Defense.
  • The shown Demon Wing odds appear to scale with wave depth and luck.
  • Using the shown 819.8% luck check as the conversion anchor, the published Demon Wing checks normalize to about 0.060% to 0.080% on a 100%-luck baseline.
  • The route is tied to the back of Punch Island in Sea 2 and uses 1 Crystal Key per run.
  • The current accessory tooltip confirms Demon Wing at 125% Defense, 102% Damage, and 15% Damage Reduction at base.
Section

How to get Demon Wing

  • Go to the back of Punch Island in Sea 2 and enter Crystal Defense.
  • Spend 1 Crystal Key to start the mode.
  • Defend the crystal and push to each 5-wave checkpoint until a random boss appears.
  • Farm those boss waves for the Demon Wing drop.
