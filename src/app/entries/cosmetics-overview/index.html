Cosmetics and Crates

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Cosmetics in Sailor Piece are stat items, not only visual items.
  • Current checked guides list seven cosmetics in the game.
  • Cosmetics come from Cosmetic Crates, which can be earned from long grinds, quests, and some live codes.

How to equip cosmetics

  • Open your inventory and move to the Cosmetics tab.
  • Pick the cosmetic you want and equip it.
  • If you want the visual model on your character, turn on the visual option too.
