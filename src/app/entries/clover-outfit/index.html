Overview

Clover Outfit

  • Clover Outfit is now visible in the Crystal Defense Defense Merchant stock.
  • The current shop capture shows Clover Outfit costing 850 Crystal Coins.
  • The item is sold by the Defense Merchant at the back of Punch Island in Sea 2.
  • The current accessory screenshot now confirms Clover Outfit at 120% Defense, 100% Damage, and 15% Damage Reduction at base.
  • The same in-game screenshot also confirms Clover Outfit reaching 170% Defense, 140% Damage, and 35% Damage Reduction at E10.
Section

How to get Clover Outfit

  • Go to the back of Punch Island in Sea 2 and enter Crystal Defense.
  • Farm Crystal Coins through the mode.
  • Talk to the Defense Merchant near the Crystal Defense entrance.
  • Buy Clover Outfit for 850 Crystal Coins.
