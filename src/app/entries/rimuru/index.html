Rimuru

Slime Island sword route tied to the slime collection puzzle, Slime Key crafting, and one of the strongest late-game boss grinds.

Overview

Rimuru

  • Rimuru is purchased from the Rimuru Seller on Slime Island after finishing the slime collection route and farming the Rimuru boss.
  • The settled requirement bundle is 30,000,000 Money, 20,000 Gems, 9 Sage Pulse, 6 Tempest Seal, 3 Slime Remnant, 1 Slime Core, and the Demon Lord title.
  • Rimuru is one of the few sword routes that naturally overlaps with a collectible puzzle because the Slime Key route starts from the Slime Puzzle.
Section

How to obtain Rimuru

  • Travel to Slime Island and complete the Slime Puzzle so the Slime Key route opens up properly.
  • Spawn and farm the Rimuru Boss until you have the full seller bundle.
  • Bring 30,000,000 Money, 20,000 Gems, 9 Sage Pulse, 6 Tempest Seal, 3 Slime Remnant, 1 Slime Core, and the Demon Lord title to the Rimuru Seller.

Where to get the required materials

  • Sage Pulse, Tempest Seal, Slime Remnant, Slime Core, and the Demon Lord title all come from the Rimuru Boss on Slime Island.
  • The Slime Puzzle is the fixed collectible route that opens the broader Slime Island progression path.
  • Money and Gems are the easier part of the grind; the boss-drop bundle is what usually takes the longest.
