Frost Relic

How to get Frost Relic in Sailor Piece, what Frost Relic is used for, and why it matters across the full Ice Queen weapon route instead of only one boss screen.

Overview

Frost Relic

  • Frost Relic is a core Ice Queen route material used for the base weapon purchase, the Ice Queen boss summon, and the Ice Queen F move mastery path.
  • The current Ice Queen page uses 25 Frost Relic for the base unlock and 85 Frost Relic for the F move mastery route.
  • Current guide wording describes Frost Relic as an any-mob, miniboss, or world-boss drop rather than an Ice Queen-only material.
Section

How to get Frost Relic

  • Current guide overlap describes Frost Relic as a broader enemy-route material rather than an Ice Queen-only boss drop.
  • Farm general enemy, miniboss, and world-boss routes while pushing toward the Ice Queen grind instead of treating Frost Relic like a single-boss material.
  • Because the route asks for so many copies, stack Frost Relics early while preparing the rest of the Ice Queen bundle.

Current Frost Relic route notes

  • Frost Relic matters twice on the Ice Queen route because you spend it both before and after getting the weapon.
  • The material is better treated like a long-term route tax than a one-time purchase item.
  • The current site does not publish one exact Frost Relic drop rate because current guides split the source across broader enemy types.
