Overview

Artifacts

Page Filter

Useful for big overview pages when you want one item fast.

  • Artifacts unlock on Snow Island after reaching level 2,500 and paying 500,000 Money plus 500 Gems.
  • Each artifact set has four slots: Helmet, Gloves, Body, and Boots.
  • Artifacts gain power through main stats, substats, set bonuses, upgrades, and milestone progression.
  • Abyssal Crown currently leads the pure damage artifact path, with Celestial Rupture close behind for damage plus sustain.
Section

How to unlock artifacts

  • Travel to Snow Island.
  • Talk to the Artifacts NPC and pay 500,000 Money plus 500 Gems once you reach level 2,500.
  • After that, artifact drops are enabled across the game.
  • The Artifact Milestone NPC stands beside the Artifacts NPC on Snow Island.

Best artifact stat priorities

  • For raw damage, start with Abyssal Crown once you are farming Ninja and Lawless NPCs or all Sea 2 NPCs consistently.
  • For a more stable all-round setup, Celestial Rupture is the best mix of damage, crit, and sustain.
  • Across strong DPS builds, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, and Damage are still the safest substats to chase first.
  • Take Damage or Crit Damage main stats whenever your slot can roll them cleanly.

Artifacts FAQ
What are artifacts in Sailor Piece?
Artifacts are a four-piece late-game gear system with set bonuses, main stats, substats, upgrades, and milestone progress.
What is the best artifact set in Sailor Piece?
Abyssal Crown is the main pure damage cap set, while Celestial Rupture is one of the best all-round sets for damage plus sustain.
Where do you unlock artifacts in Sailor Piece?
Artifacts unlock on Snow Island once you meet the level gate and pay the unlock cost to the Artifact NPC.
How do artifact upgrades work?
Artifacts are upgraded with Dust, and the final strength of a build depends on your set bonuses, piece rarity, levels, main stats, and substats.
