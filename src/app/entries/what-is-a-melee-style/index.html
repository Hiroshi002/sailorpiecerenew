Overview

What Is a Fighting Style in Sailor Piece?

  • Fighting styles are the melee combat route in Sailor Piece and are separate from fruits, swords, and the late-game spec passive system.
  • Each fighting style page should track the trainer or NPC, materials, titles, races, clans, quest steps, and any known F move or mastery route.
  • Most late-game melee routes grow stronger through titles, traits, reroll stats, artifacts, enchants, blessings, and spec passives rather than by the base style alone.
Section

How fighting styles fit into builds

  • Fighting styles are the main melee route for players who want burst combos, AoE clears, or trait-linked late-game upgrades.
  • A style can be strong on its own, but most serious melee builds are also shaped by titles, traits, races, clans, artifacts, stat rerolls, and spec passives.
  • That is why the wiki treats melee pages as progression pages instead of just move lists.
Section

What a good melee page should answer

  • Where is the trainer or NPC?
  • What exact materials, titles, races, or traits are required?
  • Is there an evolution path, domain shard route, or F move mastery requirement?
  • Which boss or dungeon loop actually feeds the build path?
